# Privacy Policy Last updated: January 27, 2026 1. Introduction Welcome to Friendship Bingo ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Friendship Bingo. 2. Information We Collect We collect information to provide a fun and social bingo experience. The types of information we collect include: Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and create a unique user ID using our backend provider, Supabase. User Content: We collect photos and videos that you voluntarily capture using the in-app camera for the purpose of gameplay (e.g., completing bingo tiles). Gameplay Data: We track your game progress, including boards completed, tiles marked, stickers unlocked, and experience points (XP). Device Information: We may collect non-personal information about your device (e.g., model, OS version) to improve app performance. 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: Create and manage your account. Enable multiplayer gameplay and lobby functionality. Sync your profile and sticker collection across devices. Generate "Memory Lane" recaps (processed locally on your device). 4. Data Storage and Security Local Data: Your "Memory Lane" history and generated recap cards are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local file storage. This specific data is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it via external apps (e.g., Instagram Stories). Cloud Data: Your user profile, current board state, and multiplayer lobby status are stored securely using Supabase. We presume standard security measures are in place to protect this data. 5. Sharing of Information We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances: With Other Players: Your display name and game status may be visible to other players in a multiplayer lobby you join. Service Providers: We use Supabase for backend services (authentication and database). Use of their services is subject to their privacy policies. 6. permissions The app requires the following permissions to function: Camera: To capture photos/videos for bingo tiles. Microphone: To capture audio for video bingo tiles. Internet: To connect to game servers and multiplayer lobbies. 7. Children's Privacy Friendship Bingo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. 8.
